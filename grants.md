#### Project Overview

This NSF EPSCoR RII Track-2 FEC project established a multidisciplinary research infrastructure for **data-driven bioengineering innovation**, integrating **machine learning, data analytics, and software systems** to accelerate materials discovery. As **Senior Personnel**, I had **lead responsibility for developing the Software Engineering (SE) thrust**, with a particular focus on **software assurance, reliability, and quality for ML-enabled systems** used in scientific research workflows.
